Post Construction Cleaning

Cleanification is your trusted partner for commercial post-construction cleaning challenges. Dust, debris, and construction remnants can impede progress, but our skilled team navigates through them effortlessly. Count on us for seamless and efficient commercial post-construction cleaning services tailored to your needs. Inspection and Planning: We develop a comprehensive plan detailing the cleaning tasks, timeline, and required resources.

Removal of Debris:

Our team removes large debris such as construction materials, packaging, and equipment, ensuring the area is clear of any hazards or obstructions..

Window and Glass Cleaning:

Windows and glass surfaces may be covered in dust, smudges, or adhesive residue. We use appropriate cleaning solutions and techniques to ensure streak-free and crystal-clear windows and glass partitions.

Dust and Surface Cleaning:

We employ various methods, including dry dusting, vacuuming, and wiping, to eliminate dust from all surfaces—walls, ceilings, floors, and fixtures. Special attention is given to high-touch areas and hard-to-reach spaces.

Floor Cleaning:

Construction projects often leave floors covered in dirt, paint splatters, and grime. We use methods such as sweeping, mopping, and scrubbing to restore floor cleanliness. Specific flooring types like carpets, hardwood, or tiles are addressed with specialized cleaning techniques.
